\subsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxyref{Ricean}{p.}{classRicean} fading model. 

This is based on the ns-2 implementation from the paper {\em Efficient Simulation of Ricean Fading within a Packet Simulator\/} by Punnoose et al. See the paper for details about the model.
