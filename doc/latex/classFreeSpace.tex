\subsection{Detailed Description}
This computes the received signal strength using the Friis free space model. 

The equation for the model $\ast$ is given by: \[ P_r = \frac{P_t G_t G_r \lambda^2}{(4\pi)^2 d^2 L} \] where $P_r$ is the receiver power, $P_t$ is the transmitted power, $G_t$ and $G_r$ are the gains of the transmitter and receiver antennas, respectively, $\lambda$ is the wavelength, $d$ is the distance between the transmitter and receiver, and $L$ is the loss factor.
